Philippines

People

Filipino people are known for their warmth, hospitality, and strong family ties. Despite facing challenges, Filipinos are admired for their resilience and cheerful outlook on life, often expressed through their love of music, festivals, and shared laughter.

Islands

The Philippines is an archipelago of over 7,600 islands, known for its stunning natural beauty and diverse landscapes. From pristine white-sand beaches like those in Boracay and Palawan to lush mountains and rice terraces in Banaue, the islands showcase a variety of breathtaking sceneries.

Food

Filipino food is a flavorful blend of indigenous, Asian, Spanish, and American influences, creating a cuisine that’s rich, diverse, and comforting. It is characterized by the balance of sweet, sour, salty, and savory flavors, often combined in one dish.
